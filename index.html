Our store in Damascus is one of the largest and most prestigious perfume retailers in the Middle East and the world, distinguished by numerous features, the most notable of which are:

  • A wide variety of Oriental and Western perfumes.
  • The ability to purchase our products online through our official website.
  • A genuine warranty on all products, covering both storage-related issues and manufacturer guarantees.

Our perfumes are carefully and passionately selected to suit your personality at all times, occasions, and throughout all seasons—summer, spring, autumn, and winter. All you need to do is provide us with these details, and we will guide you to the section featuring the fragrances that best match your preferences.

Why Choose Musk & Oud

Because it takes into account the individual fragrance preferences of each customer, and provides a warranty card with every item. This card can also be given to a friend, who can then visit our store and receive a special discount upon making a purchase. Additionally, the original customer will receive the same discount on their next purchase.
